### Key Technical Insight
- Svelte 5 `$effect` cleanup is critical for DOM side effects like `document.body.style.overflow` — without the return cleanup function, navigating away from admin leaves the body scroll-locked.
- `position: sticky` inside a flex container with `overflow: hidden auto` only works if the sticky element's siblings don't have their own `overflow-y: auto` — otherwise the sibling scrolls internally and the parent never scrolls, making sticky a no-op. Fix: override child's overflow to `visible` on mobile.
- `inert` on a sidebar that's always visible on desktop but toggled on mobile requires `matchMedia` tracking — you can't just bind it to `!open` because that makes the sidebar inert on desktop where `open` is never set.
